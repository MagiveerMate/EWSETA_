About Us

Who We Are

The Energy and Water Sector Education and Training Authority (EWSETA) is one of South Africa's 21 SETAs. Established in terms of Section 9 of the Skills Development Act (SDA), 97 of 1998, as amended, EWSETA reports to the Honourable Minister of Higher Education, Science and Technology. The purpose of this portal is to educate website visitors about the diverse career opportunities available in the Energy and Water sector in South Africa.

What We Do

OUR SECTORS

SA's Water Sector

South Africa's water sector is dedicated to providing access to clean and safe water for all citizens.

SA's Energy Sector

South Africa's energy sector is committed to providing reliable and affordable energy to support the country's economic growth and development.

Why We Built This Website

Our commitment lies in collaborating with stakeholders nationwide, including government, businesses, and communities, to address the challenges our water and energy systems face. We firmly believe that by working together, we can create a brighter and more sustainable future for South Africa.

We take pride in the progress we have made in providing access to clean water and energy for all South Africans. As we move forward, we remain dedicated to ensuring that every person has access to these essential resources, and we will continue to work towards that goal.

Psychometric Tests

Struggling to decide which career path to take as a high school student? With numerous options available, the process can be overwhelming and confusing. Fortunately, psychometric testing can assist you in this regard.

Psychometric tests can help you identify your strengths, weaknesses, interests, and personality traits, enabling you to determine the best career paths for you. By providing valuable insights into your abilities and skills, these tests equip you with the knowledge and confidence you need to make informed decisions about your future.
